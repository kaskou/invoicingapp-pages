Disconnecting Invoicing App

To disconnect the Invoicing App from your QuickBooks Online company:

  1. Open the Invoicing App on your computer.
  2. Go to Settings in the sidebar.
  3. Under QuickBooks Connection, click Disconnect.
  4. Confirm the disconnection in the dialog.

The app will immediately delete the OAuth tokens stored on your machine. No further API calls will be made to QuickBooks Online from this installation.

Alternative: You can also revoke access from the QuickBooks Online side by signing in to your QuickBooks Online company, going to Settings → Apps, finding "Invoicing App", and clicking Disconnect.
