Invoicing App

Private QuickBooks Online integration developed by Kaushik Sangem

About

This is a private, single-tenant desktop application that integrates with QuickBooks Online to streamline invoice generation for one specific Australian business. It is not available to the public.

How to launch

  1. Open the Invoicing App on the User's Windows machine.
  2. On first run, go to Settings → Connect to QuickBooks.
  3. Authorise the app against the User's QuickBooks Online company.
  4. Once connected, use the New Submission screen to generate invoices.
