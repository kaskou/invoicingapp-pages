Reconnecting Invoicing App

If your Invoicing App connection to QuickBooks Online has been disconnected, follow these steps to reconnect:

  1. Open the Invoicing App on your computer.
  2. Go to Settings in the sidebar.
  3. Under QuickBooks Connection, click Connect to QuickBooks.
  4. Your default browser will open. Sign in to your QuickBooks Online account if asked.
  5. Choose the QuickBooks Online company you want to connect, then click Connect.
  6. Once authorisation is complete, you can close the browser tab and return to the app.
Common reasons a reconnect is needed:
  • The OAuth refresh token expired (refresh tokens last about 100 days of inactivity).
  • The connection was manually disconnected, either inside the app or from QuickBooks Online.
  • You changed which QuickBooks Online company you want to use.
  • QuickBooks Online password or two-factor settings changed and existing tokens were invalidated.
